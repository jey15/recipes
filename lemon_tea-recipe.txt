Ingredients:
 - 1 cup water(according to people)
 - 1/4 teaspoon salt
 - 1 tablespoon sugar(1 tablespoon of sugar for each cup of water)
 - 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
 - 1 indian bay leaf
 - 1 teaspoon tea leaves

Directions:
 1. Heat water in a saucepan.
 
 2. Add salt, sugar and bay leaf to the water and bring the water to a boil.
 
 3. When the water starts boiling, add tea leaves. Leave for 15 seconds and switch off the flame.

 4. Add lemon juice.

 5. Filter the tea and pour it a cup. It is now ready to serve.
